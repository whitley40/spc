Contact SPC Today

Dr. Duncan Simpson, the founder of Simpson Performance Consulting is a renowned expert in the field of human performance and is based in Miami, FL. His unique and sophisticated approach allows clients to find their optimal potential, while increasing their quality of life outside of their performance domain.

Dr Duncan Simpson

As a two-sport (tennis & soccer) college athlete I’ve long had an interest in the psychology behind performance. I always felt my own performances were limited far more by my psychological skills than by my physical abilities. Following the end of my own athletic career I was driven in the pursuit of helping others achieve their potential. A master’s degree in Sport and Exercise Science from Leeds Metropolitan in the UK (Now Leeds Beckett University) and numerous coaching positions have given me a strong foundation with which to address a plethora of performance issues. To further my education I completed my PhD in Sport Psychology at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville under the supervision of world-renowned Sport Psychologist – Dr. Craig Wrisberg (Professor Emeritus).

As well as my educational background I am a Certified Consultant with the Association of Applied Sport Psychology. This certification acknowledges I have met specific educational requirements in sport and exercise psychology and have completed an extensive, supervised consulting experience. Since 2005 I have been working with athletes, performers, and teams to assist them in achieving their optimal performance.

SPC was formed out of a love and passion to help individuals and teams perform at the highest level. I strongly believe the goals of SPC are consistent with those of individuals and teams wishing to reach their fullest potential. While improved performance is always our primary goal we simultaneously guide clients to become competent, ethical, and socially conscious professionals and leaders. SPC enjoys working in vibrant environments and with colleagues from diverse backgrounds and experiences.

Personal
